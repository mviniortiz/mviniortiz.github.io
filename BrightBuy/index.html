Business Intelligence Project: BrightBuy

Interactive sales performance dashboard analyzing key business metrics including revenue, profit margins, customer segmentation, and vendor performance

Back to Portfolio
View Live Dashboard

Project Overview

This project focused on developing a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard for BrightBuy, a retail company looking to gain deeper insights into their sales performance across multiple product categories and vendors.

The goal was to create an intuitive, interactive dashboard that would allow stakeholders to analyze revenue trends, identify high-performing products, optimize inventory management, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.

Key Features

Multi-dimensional Analysis

Comprehensive view of sales data from multiple perspectives including time periods, product categories, customer segments, and geographical regions.

Interactive Filtering

Dynamic filters and slicers allowing users to drill down into specific data segments for detailed analysis of performance metrics and trends.

Customer Segmentation

Advanced segmentation of customer data to identify high-value customers, analyze purchasing patterns, and develop targeted marketing strategies.

Profitability Analysis

Detailed profit margin analysis by product, category, and vendor to identify areas for cost optimization and revenue enhancement.

Technical Details

  • Data Integration: Connected multiple data sources including sales transactions, product catalog, customer information, and vendor data into a unified data model.
  • ETL Process: Implemented robust ETL processes to clean, transform, and normalize data for advanced analysis.
  • Advanced DAX: Created complex DAX measures and calculated columns to derive business metrics and KPIs.
  • Interactive Elements: Implemented slicers, filters, and bookmarks for enhanced user interaction and deeper data exploration.

Business Impact

  • Sales Optimization: Increased sales by 15% through better identification of top-performing products and focused marketing efforts.
  • Inventory Management: Reduced stockouts by 40% and excess inventory costs by 25% through improved demand forecasting.
  • Vendor Performance: Optimized supplier relationships by identifying the most reliable and cost-effective vendors, improving delivery times by 30%.
  • Decision Making: Reduced decision-making time for executive leadership by 50% through readily available, actionable insights.
