Sales Analysis: Case Project

Comprehensive sales analysis dashboard focusing on product performance and using the Pareto principle to identify key products

Project Overview

This sales analysis dashboard was developed to provide comprehensive insights into product performance, revenue trends, and customer purchasing behavior. By applying the Pareto principle (80/20 rule), the dashboard identifies the vital few products that contribute the majority of revenue, allowing for focused inventory and marketing strategies.

Key Features

Pareto Analysis

Implementation of the 80/20 rule to identify the top 20% of products that generate 80% of revenue, helping focus resources on high-value items.

Temporal Analysis

Multi-level time series analysis showing daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly sales trends to identify seasonality and growth patterns.

Product Category Analysis

Detailed breakdown of sales performance by product categories and subcategories, highlighting the most profitable product lines.

Customer Segmentation

Analysis of purchasing patterns across different customer segments, revealing key differences in product preferences and buying behavior.

Geographical Distribution

Geographic visualization of sales performance across regions, identifying high-performing and underperforming markets.

KPI Tracking

Comprehensive set of key performance indicators including total revenue, units sold, average order value, and year-over-year growth.

Technical Details

  • Data Integration: Unified 9 different tables into a single fact table to enable complex measure creation and analysis.
  • Data Modeling: Implemented a star schema design with dimension tables for products, customers, time, and geography.
  • Data Transformation: Used Python for advanced data cleaning, normalization, and feature engineering.
  • Visualization: Created visually impactful graphics using a combination of Figma for design elements and Power BI for interactive dashboards.
  • DAX Measures: Developed complex DAX formulas for Pareto analysis, rolling averages, and custom KPI calculations.

Business Impact

  • Inventory Optimization: Reduced inventory costs by 18% by focusing on high-value items identified through Pareto analysis.
  • Marketing Efficiency: Improved marketing ROI by reallocating budget to focus on top-performing product categories.
  • Revenue Growth: Contributed to a 12% increase in sales by identifying and capitalizing on cross-selling opportunities.
  • Customer Insights: Enhanced customer segmentation strategies leading to more targeted product offerings and increased customer satisfaction.
