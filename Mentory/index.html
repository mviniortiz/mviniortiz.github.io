Marketing Campaign Analysis: Mentory

Interactive marketing performance dashboard for analyzing KPIs from multiple advertising platforms

Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

This project focused on developing an interactive marketing performance dashboard for Mentory using Looker Studio to analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) from multiple advertising platforms including Google Ads, TikTok Ads, and Facebook Ads.

The goal was to provide real-time monitoring of campaign performance, enabling the marketing team to make data-driven decisions and optimize their advertising strategy across different platforms.

Key Features

Multi-platform Analytics

Consolidated view of performance metrics across Google Ads, TikTok Ads, and Facebook Ads with unified KPI tracking and campaign comparison.

Real-time Monitoring

Live dashboard updates with automated data refreshes, allowing the marketing team to track campaign performance as it happens and respond quickly to trends.

UTM Tracking

Custom UTM parameter tracking system to accurately attribute conversions and engagement to specific campaigns, ads, and channels throughout the customer journey.

Performance Indicators

Visual cards and indicators showing the health of campaigns with color-coded status markers and threshold alerts for key metrics like CPC, CTR, and ROAS.

Key Performance Indicators

Cost per Acquisition

Tracking the cost efficiency of ad spend across platforms with comparison to benchmarks

Click-Through Rate

Analyzing ad engagement and effectiveness with historical trend analysis

Conversion Rate

Measuring the percentage of clicks that result in conversions with segmentation by platform

Return on Ad Spend

Calculating the revenue generated for every dollar spent on advertising

Technologies Used

The dashboard was developed using a combination of powerful data analysis and visualization tools:

Looker Studio Google Sheets Python API Integrations Figma

Business Impact

The Mentory marketing dashboard has transformed the company's advertising strategy:

  • Enabled the marketing team to make faster, data-driven decisions by providing real-time performance metrics
  • Improved campaign ROI by 32% through better budget allocation based on performance insights
  • Increased conversion rates by identifying and optimizing underperforming ad creatives and targeting parameters
  • Reduced time spent on manual reporting by 85%, freeing up the team to focus on strategic planning
